All About ATM

What is ATM?

ATM stands for applied technology magnet. You have to do ATM for your threes years of middle school. During my years in ATM I've learned multiple skills.In sixth grade I learned multiple skills like learning how to type. Learning how to type made a huge impact on my school work because if a task in class requires me to type I can get a lot accomplished during class time.Internet saftey is something you learn in sixth grade to help protect you from dangerous people who search for innocent kids on the internet. I learned how to take my social media off of the internet for anyone to see. In seventh grade I was introduced many different magnets like Floor planner, Photoshop, photography, and sketchup. In sketchup I was able to work with a group of three for a project. This assignment helped me understand what it takes to work with a group other than yourself. Photoshop showed me how to be creative with my work other than having a simple picture or font for a flyer or projects in my future. Photography allowed me to edit and change up the vibe of a picture. This unit really helped me by showing me how to properly take a picture to show effection to people, so they can relate. Floor planner really helped me understand how much time and effort it takes to build something as simple as a classroom.
